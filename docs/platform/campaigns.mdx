Campaigns run codemods across one or more repositories. Codemod creates tasks, opens pull requests, tracks CI checks, and gives you one place to follow the rollout.

Use a campaign when you want to move from "we found the work" to "we are applying the change".

## Campaign flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a campaign">
    Choose a registry codemod and select one or more target repositories.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the plan">
    Confirm the package, repositories, campaign name, arguments, and recovery settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track the rollout">
    Watch workflow tasks, generated pull requests, and CI checks from the campaign detail page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
